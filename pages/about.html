About Our Daycare Center

Welcome to our nurturing and playful daycare environment!

What your child learns throughout the day reinforces what he or she is being taught at home. At Daystar Daycare Center, we look for teachable moments as your child plays, helping your child learn all day long. Our Discovering Me curriculum focuses on six areas of development:

  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Cognitive Development
  • Physical Development and Health
  • Language and Communication
  • Creative Expression and Sensory
  • Outdoor Learning and Nature

Our Mission

At our daycare center, we are dedicated to providing a safe, loving, and stimulating environment for children to learn, play, and grow.

Our Values

We uphold the following core values:

  • Nurturing and caring atmosphere
  • Respect for each child's unique qualities
  • Excellence in early childhood education
  • Collaboration with families and community

Our Goals

  • Provide a nurturing and secure environment for every child.
  • Foster a love for learning through play-based activities.
  • Promote social and emotional development through positive interactions.
  • Partner with families to support each child's individual needs and growth.
